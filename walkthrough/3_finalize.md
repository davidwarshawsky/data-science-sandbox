# Step 3: Finalize & Prove

After running your experiments and writing to `output/`:

1.  Run the **Finalize** command.
2.  We will **Hash** your inputs and outputs.
3.  We will **Sign** the manifest with your GPG key.
4.  We will **Timestamp** it with an external authority.
